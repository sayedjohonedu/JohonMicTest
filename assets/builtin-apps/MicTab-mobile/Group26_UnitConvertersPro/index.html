📏 Circumference Measurement Guide

Inner Circumference ≈ Diameter × π

1️⃣ Wrap a strip of paper around your finger at the knuckle
2️⃣ Mark where the paper overlaps
3️⃣ Measure the length in mm — this is your circumference
4️⃣ Diameter = Circumference ÷ 3.14159
